How to Play
1
Grab your phone and go to picit.tvEnter the room code on the TV and type your name
2
A prompt appears each round — like "Pizza" or "Something adorable"Pick the best photo from your camera roll in 60 seconds
3
All photos are revealed on the TV one by one — no names yet!Then everyone votes for their favorite on their phone
4
Most votes after 10 rounds wins!You can't vote for your own photo — choose wisely
